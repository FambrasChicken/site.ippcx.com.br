DECLARATION OF EXCLUSIVITY
Attest to the exclusivity of your product and service in the international market

It is a document that attests to the exclusivity of the product or service and its main objective is to claim the waiver of bidding before public agencies under the justification of non-enforceability when there is no competition.

And when there are no competitors for the bidding to take place properly, it is extremely important to declare and prove the exclusivity of a supplier. This is when the supplier exclusivity letter enters, which must prove that there are no competitors in this tender and that the chosen company – in addition to being reputable – must prove that it will be able to fulfill as promised.

Ippex is entitled to issue the declaration of exclusivity for companies associated with the Faciap System.
